About

Solar Carport

Welcome to the epitome of innovation in sustainable transportation - the Solar Carport Charging Station. Revolutionising the way we power electric vehicles, these stations merge the potency of solar energy with the convenience of vehicle charging, creating a harmonious synergy between clean power generation and eco-friendly transportation.

What is it?

Picture a sleek, modern structure that not only shelters vehicles from the elements but also harnesses the sun's abundant energy through photovoltaic panels adorning its canopy. These stations are intelligently designed to capture sunlight and convert it into electricity, which is then used to charge electric vehicles parked beneath.

Versatile Design Easy!

The design versatility of solar carport stations allows them to be integrated seamlessly into various settings, including commercial parking lots, residential areas, and public spaces, contributing to an aesthetic and functional enhancement of the surroundings.

How we organise your project

1. Survey the site

2. Setup and build

3. Final inspection

4. Approved for use

Benefits

Offering a convenient and readily available charging solution, these stations enhance the accessibility and practicality of electric vehicles for drivers, ensuring they can charge while parked or during their daily routines.

Excess energy generated by the solar panels can be stored in batteries or redirected to the power grid, furthering the cause of energy independence and sustainability.
